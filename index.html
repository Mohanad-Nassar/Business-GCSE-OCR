FAQ

Good questions

How do I join my class?
Create an account (email or Google), then enter the 8-character class code your teacher gives you. Studying more than one subject here? Redeem one code per subject.
Is it free?
For the 2026–27 year the platform is provided free to our school's students. School enquiries are welcome via the contact details in the footer.
How does the spaced repetition work?
The first time you practise a topic, reviews are scheduled after 1 day, 1 week and 4 weeks. Each review is a short quiz — pass it and the topic is ticked off until its next stage; fail and the cycle restarts. It's the pattern memory research keeps landing on.
Can I cheat by peeking at the answers?
No — practice questions are graded on the server and the correct answer only comes back after you've submitted. (We're teachers. We thought of it.)
Does it work on my phone?
Yes — every page is responsive, including the calendar and daily practice. A laptop is comfier for long written answers.
What happens to my data?
Progress data is stored securely and used only to power your revision and your teacher's view of it. See the privacy policy linked below for the full picture.
