Examiner's Note — What to Watch Out For
The Marketing Mix is one of the most heavily tested areas in the paper. Students most often lose marks by confusing price skimming with penetration pricing (skimming starts high, penetration starts low), and by failing to use data actively in extended answers — always calculate a percentage change rather than just quoting figures from the source. High-tariff questions (like the 9-mark Walkers question) reward students who link all 4 Ps together rather than treating them in isolation.
