For pupils, in plain English

We keep track of which questions you get right and wrong so we can show you what to practise next, and how many days in a row you've revised. Only you and your teacher can see this — it's never shown to other pupils, and it's never used to advertise anything to you. You can ask your teacher or a parent/carer to help you see, fix, or delete your data whenever you want.

1. Why this page exists

Because this Platform is used by pupils under 18, we follow the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) Age Appropriate Design Code (the "Children's Code"). This page explains, in age-appropriate language, what that means in practice for this site.

2. High-privacy defaults

  • Your progress and answers are private by default — visible only to you and your class teacher(s), never to other pupils.
  • There is no public profile, public leaderboard, or public sharing of your results.
  • We don't use your data for advertising, and we don't sell or share it with marketing companies.
  • We don't build a profile of you beyond showing you your own revision progress.

3. What we track, explained simply

  • Which questions you get right or wrong — so your dashboard can show you which topics to revise more.
  • Your day-streak — how many days in a row you've done some revision, to help build a regular habit.
  • Badges and levels — worked out from your progress each time you look at your dashboard, not stored as a separate "score" anywhere.

None of this is shared outside the Platform, and none of it is used to decide anything about you other than what to show on your own revision dashboard.

4. Designed to help you revise, not to keep you online

Streaks, XP and badges are there to encourage steady, healthy revision habits — not to keep you on the site longer than is useful. There are no push notifications, no autoplay, and no infinite-scroll style features designed to maximise your time on the Platform.

5. Your rights, explained simply

You — or a parent/carer or teacher helping you — can ask us at any time to:

  • Show you what data we hold about you.
  • Fix anything that's wrong.
  • Delete your data.

Just email mohanadmaeen.edu@gmail.com, or ask your teacher to pass on the request. See our full Privacy Policy for the detailed legal version of all of this.
