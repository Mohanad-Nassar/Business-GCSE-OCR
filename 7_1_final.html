The 9-Mark Question — How It's Always Marked

1
KNOWLEDGE
Define the change that makes things happen (e.g. define marketing)
1
UNDERSTANDING
Explain the concept — what activities does it include?
2
APPLICATION
Use TWO pieces of case study evidence to illustrate the link
2
ANALYSIS
Explain the effect/impact on the second business area
3
EVALUATION
Judgement (1) + Justified with context (2). Must refer to BOTH areas if question names two.
