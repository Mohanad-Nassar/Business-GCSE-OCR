New Task

1Details & settings

Only the title and due date are required — the other defaults suit a normal homework. You can edit everything while it's a draft.

Multiple-choice and True/False are always marked automatically. Written answers go to your marking queue.

2Choose questions

Click a topic to expand it, then click a category (📚 Key Learning, ❓ MCQ Quiz, 🔗 Matching, ✏️ Fill the Blanks, ⚠️ Misconceptions, 🎯 Exam Tips, ✅ True/False, 📝 Exam Practice) to see its questions — tick exactly which ones you want, or set a number and click 🎲 Add random to let the system pick that many from just that category. Use 👁 Preview to read a question in full before adding it. Everything you add appears in the preview on the right and is kept in the same topic-then-category order students will answer it in.

3Assign to classes & students

Per-student deadline override and extra time are for absences and SEN/extra-time accommodations. Leave blank to use the task defaults.
