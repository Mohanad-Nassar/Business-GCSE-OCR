ℹ️ How the Vocab Lab works

Every word tracks its own status as you practise, using the same colours as your flashcards:

New — not yet practised, or you're still finding it tricky.   Practising — getting there, one correct answer in.   Known — two or more correct in a row.

Every activity below reads and updates the same word-by-word progress, and leans a little more on the words you're still practising — so switching activities never resets what you've learned. Progress is saved on this device.
